STORY

> HOW CAELYS BEGAN

CAELYS began with a simple frustration: why did the most intellectually ambitious students in Jodhpur have to leave the city to find the experiences they deserved?

Disha Jariwala and Supriya Vishnoi, both veterans of the national MUN and debate circuit, decided to build the platform they wished had existed when they were starting out — one that was rigorous without being exclusionary, competitive without being toxic, and rooted in Jodhpur without being limited by it.

CAELYS 2027 is the result of over two years of planning, research, and an unshakeable belief that the next generation of Indian leaders are already in our schools and colleges — they just need the right room to walk into.

PURPOSE

"To build India's most intellectually honest youth summit — where curiosity is rewarded, depth is valued, and every mind that walks in leaves bigger than when it arrived."

> OUR VISION

A Jodhpur where the most driven young minds have a world-class platform to engage, compete, and grow — without having to leave home to find it.

> OUR MISSION

To design experiences — in diplomacy, rhetoric, and climate — that push students beyond performance into genuine understanding, and beyond understanding into action.

CORE PHILOSOPHY

Intellectual Honesty

We believe in engaging with ideas on their merits — not just debating to win, but to understand.

Collaborative Growth

Learning happens best in community. CAELYS is designed so every participant grows through the growth of others.

Empathy First

Before rhetoric, we teach listening. Before solutions, we teach understanding the problem deeply.

Excellence Without Ego

We celebrate achievement but never at the expense of the person beside you. Healthy competition, always.

Action-Oriented

Ideas without outcomes are incomplete. Every vertical at CAELYS is designed to produce something tangible.

Lifelong Learning

CAELYS is not a finish line. It is a beginning. We want every delegate to leave hungrier than they arrived.

DIFFERENT BY DESIGN

01

Deliberately Small

CAELYS is not designed to be the biggest summit. It is designed to be the most impactful. Every delegate gets real attention, real feedback, and a real experience.

02

Three Verticals, One Philosophy

Our verticals are not separate events. They share a common philosophy of depth over breadth, and outcome over performance.

03

Founder-Led

CAELYS is built and run by young people — not a corporate entity. Every decision is made with the delegate experience at the centre.

04

City-First

Jodhpur deserves a world-class intellectual platform. CAELYS is that platform — built here, for here, with global ambition.
